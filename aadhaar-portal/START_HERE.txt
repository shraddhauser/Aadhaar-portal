════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. CSV → DATABASE
   • Parses 12,500 records from 3 CSV files
   • Cleans and normalizes data (gender mapping, age grouping, etc.)
   • Validates all records against schema requirements
   • Inserts into MySQL with proper relationships

2. REAL-TIME ANALYTICS
   • Live enrollment counts by gender/age/region
   • Status distribution (approved/rejected/pending)
   • Regional performance tracking
   • Biometric quality metrics

3. PREDICTIVE MODELS
   • 3-month enrollment forecast (exponential smoothing)
   • Growth pattern identification by region
   • Trend analysis with confidence intervals

4. ANOMALY DETECTION
   • Detects low biometric quality (< 50%)
   • Identifies high rejection rates (> 15%)
   • Flags unusual patterns automatically
   • Real-time alerts in dashboard

5. HISTORICAL ANALYSIS
   • Day-by-day enrollment trends
   • Regional comparison over time
   • Monthly and quarterly reports
   • Year-over-year growth metrics
