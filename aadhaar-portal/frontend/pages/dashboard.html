Project Overview
Submitted by Shreyas, Shraddha, Abid, Zail

This portal analyses Aadhaar enrolment, demographic, and biometric datasets to uncover societal trends and anomalies. It integrates live statistics, historical trend analysis, and an AI-powered recommendation framework — supporting data-driven decisions for policymakers and UIDAI administrators.
